About ASTS Uganda

A Smart Transport System Uganda is the nation's leading AI-driven mobility platform, dedicated to safe, reliable, and accessible transport for all citizens.

Our Mission

To revolutionize urban and rural mobility in Uganda by integrating cutting-edge GPS tracking, AI-powered dispatching, and secure cashless payments into a unified, professional transport network.

Core Values

Safety First

Every driver is verified, and every trip is monitored by our 24/7 Security Command Center.

Fair Pricing

Eliminating overcharging through transparent, GPS-calculated distance-based fares.

Innovation

Using predictive AI to reduce wait times and optimize routes across the Pearl of Africa.
