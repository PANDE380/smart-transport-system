About STS Uganda

A transport platform built for safer, clearer city movement.

STS Uganda brings booking, payments, driver verification, and live operations into one reliable system that works for passengers, drivers, and transport teams.

Our mission

We are building a transport experience that feels organized, traceable, and dependable from the first booking step to the final payment and feedback screen.

  • Clear trip pricing and route visibility
  • Verified drivers and managed vehicle records
  • Accessible booking through web and USSD

What STS solves

Public and on demand transport often feels fragmented. STS connects dispatch, service information, payments, and safety workflows so users are not moving between separate tools.

  • One interface for taxis, bodas, buses, minibuses, and marine travel
  • Shared operations view for admin and driver teams
  • Consistent support and reporting flow after each trip

Principles behind the product

Professional transport systems earn trust when the experience stays simple, transparent, and accountable.

Verified operations

Drivers, vehicles, and trip records are structured so approvals and reviews are easy to follow.

Safety first

SOS, trip visibility, and support flows are kept close to the booking journey rather than hidden away.

Clear payments

Wallet balances, top ups, and spending summaries are visible in one place so riders always know where they stand.

Access for more users

USSD support keeps booking and service information available even when a smartphone or data plan is not available.

How the platform works

1. Booking and service selection Passengers choose a service and enter a route.
2. Matching and live updates The system surfaces nearby availability and route details.
3. Payment and trip history Wallet activity and ride records stay connected.
4. Oversight and support Driver, admin, and support tools close the loop.
