Safety and trust

Safety is built into the booking flow, not added at the end.

STS keeps driver verification, trip tracking, SOS access, and support close to every ride so passengers and drivers can act quickly when something feels wrong.

Safety controls across the platform

These controls are visible in booking, trip management, and support workflows so users do not need to search for them.

Driver and vehicle checks

Accounts, license details, and vehicle information are collected before driver approval and service activation.

Trip visibility

Pickup points, routes, and vehicle movement stay visible to help riders and operators understand what is happening in real time.

SOS actions

Emergency alerts can be triggered quickly from the main experience and tied back to current trip activity.

Post trip support

Ratings, support tickets, and lost item reporting help keep accountability in place after the ride ends.

Passenger checklist

  • Confirm the driver and vehicle details before the trip begins.
  • Review the route and fare estimate before sending a booking request.
  • Use the in app support and rating tools if the experience needs review.
  • Use SOS immediately if you believe you are in danger.

Need help right now?

The safety tools are available directly from the main ride flow and support screens.
